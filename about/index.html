Hey there! I’m a visual designer born in Romania, raised in Belgium, and currently calling Amsterdam home (yes, I bike everywhere now).

I have a bachelor in Digital Design and Development and a master in Digital Design. I have previous experience working as a UX/UI designer at Accelerate Studio in Brussels. I like to think of myself as a creative problem-solver with a soft spot for bold ideas and designs that break the mold—without breaking the user experience. My goal? To make digital experiences that are meaningful, a little unexpected, and occasionally smile-inducing. I believe great design should feel like a good surprise, like finding fries at the bottom of the bag :) If it educates, entertains, or makes someone pause (in a good way), I’m doing something right.
