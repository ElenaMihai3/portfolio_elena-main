Where tutors and students connect

Result

Eduvik is a platform for all-round communication between tutors and tutees. In the platform tutors can manage their lessons, finalize payments, manage their tutees and so on. the briefing was to design and re-think the user experience to ensure a better process which is intuitive and efficient for both tutors and learners.

Next to the platform we also designed their promotional website using a coherent branding that effectively converted parents into users.

Challenge

Create a platform for all-round communication between 3000+ tutors and tutees. Additionally, design a promotional website to convert users.

The challenge here was finding a UX-friendly flow in managing a lot of data for the tutors and finding a easy flow to manage multiple tutees at the same time.
